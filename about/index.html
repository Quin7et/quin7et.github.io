About

| Jun 7, 2022

I am Quin7et (pronounced “quintet”), a writer and a game developer.

I made Campfire for Ludum Dare 50. I translated this Devil Daggers guide by Braden. I have translated a few entries (into Chinese) on the SCP Foundation. I wrote a short fiction published on Science Fiction World when I was 17. You’ve probably noticed that I didn’t link it. The reason being that I am not proud of it.

I enjoy science fictions that don’t involve space (or at least don’t make a big deal of it). I use C# for most of my projects. I believe good characters and plot is more important than setting. My favorite musician is Shostakovich. I find it terrifying that Cyberpunk is still relevant today after 40 years. I am an INTP, if it at all matters. I think capitalism is built upon a depressing premise and we, as a compassionate specie, can do better.

Cereal is not soup. Hotdogs are not sandwich. Mid-life crisis is just mid-development hell but irl.
