About Me

I am Quin Li (Quin7et), level designer/tech designer/indie dev.

I made Campfire for Ludum Dare 50. I translated this Devil Daggers guide by Braden. I have translated a few entries (into Chinese) on the SCP Foundation. I wrote a short fiction published on Science Fiction World when I was 17.

I enjoy science fictions that don’t involve space (or at least don’t make a big deal of it). I use C# for most of my projects. My favorite musician is Shostakovich. I find it terrifying that Cyberpunk is still relevant today after 40 years.
